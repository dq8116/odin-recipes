Simple Deep Fried Turkey

Description

Learn to deep fry a turkey.

Ingredients

  • Peanut oil
  • Turkey
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large stockpot to 350 degrees F.
  2. Make sure turkey is thawed. Pat dry. Place in basket.
  3. Lower basket into stockpot slowly. Cook turkey for 3.5 minutes per pound.
  4. Remove turkey from stockpot. Rest for 15 min.
